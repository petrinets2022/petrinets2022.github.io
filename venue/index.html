Conference Venue

The conference will take place in the K2 building at Campus Bergen of Western Norway University of Applied Science. You can easily reach the building from either Bergen city centre or Bergen airport by tram (light-rail)

The conference venue / Campus is just next to the stop “Kronstad”.
