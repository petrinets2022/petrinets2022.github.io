Registration

Please register with the online registration form (switch to English in the top-right corner).

Registration for ICTAC 2020 is free through support from Springer and the Norwegian Research Council.

At least one author per accepted paper must register to the conference by Monday, 18th November.

The conference and the RV lectures will be implemented using Zoom.

2 x 1/2 day RV Lectures on 30th Nov – 1st Dec is for free, please indicate in the registration page about your interest.

Detailed information about how to access the virtual conference will be sent to the registered participants ahead of the conference.
