Main Track

The main conference takes place from Wednesday 22 to Friday 24. The three days before the main conference also offer a wide range of activities.

It is also possible to arrange meetings and courses related to Petri Nets and concurrency. Submissions for such activities must contain a 2-5 page description. They must be received by the Workshops chairs via email no later than January 13, 2022.
