PETRI NETS 2022

43rd International Conference on Application and Theory of Petri Nets and Concurrency
June 19 - 24, 2022, Bergen, Norway

Welcome to Petri Nets 2022

The 43rd International Conference on Application and Theory of Petri Nets and Concurrency with affiliated workshops, advanced tutorials, and the Petri nets PhD course will take place in Bergen, Norway on 19 June - 24 June, 2022.

News

  • Call for Tool Demonstrations - deadline June 10

The conference is organised by the Software Engineering Research Group at Western Norway University of Applied Sciences and will be held at Campus Bergen.
