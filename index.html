43rd International Conferance on Application and Theory of Petri Nets and Concurrency
June 19 - 24, 2022, Bergen, Norway

Welcome to Petri Nets 2022

The 43rd International Conference on Application and Theory of Petri Nets and Concurrency will take place in Bergen, Norway on 19 June - 24 June, 2022.

The conference is organised by the Software Engineering Research Group at Western Norway University of Applied Sciences and will be held at Campus bergen

The city of Bergen

Bergen is a world heritage city and the gateway to the Fjords of Norway. The city is well-connected to all major European flight hubs.
