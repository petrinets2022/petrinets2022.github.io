PETRI NETS 2022

43rd International Conference on Application and Theory of Petri Nets and Concurrency
June 19 - 24, 2022, Bergen, Norway

Welcome to Petri Nets 2022

The 43rd International Conference on Application and Theory of Petri Nets and Concurrency and affiliated workshops, advanced tutorials, and Petri netts PhD course will take place in Bergen, Norway on 19 June - 24 June, 2022.

Submission of papers to one of the affiliated workshops

  • Petri Nets and Software Engineering (PNSE'22): https://www2.informatik.uni-hamburg.de/TGI/events/pnse/
  • Algorithms and Theories for the Analysis of Event Data (ATAED'22): https://www.fernuni-hagen.de/ataed/ataed2022/
  • International Health Data Workshop (HEDA'22): https://cs.ttu.ee/events/heda-2022/

The conference is organised by the Software Engineering Research Group at Western Norway University of Applied Sciences and will be held at Campus Bergen.
